What does he do?

Max is a member of the Tri-M Music Honor Society and SFP Chamber Orchestra, playing at most events for the school. He also has maintained a spot on the Principals List for all four quarters during junior year. Recently, he and his friend were chosen and inducted into the Computer Science Honor Society. Coding is a passion of his, and he's learning more and more about it as you read this.
